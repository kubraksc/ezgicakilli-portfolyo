About me

I started art at Çanakkale Art Theater in 2007. I won the Painting Department at Hüseyin Akif Terzioğlu High School. I participated in the exhibition from oil painting and sculpture in high school. After graduating from high school, I returned to theater in 2017 and acted on stages. At the same time, I continued to improve myself in drawing for 4 years at Kule Art Workshop.


In 2021, I started to study animation at Anima School. When my course was over, I played an active role in animations as a trainee. My first work experience was at Anadolu University Pre-rootstock Incubation Center. I worked for 8 months.
